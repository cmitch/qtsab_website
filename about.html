About Us

Queer/Trans South Asians at Berkeley is a student group at UC Berkeley dedicated to fostering a safe space for students of South Asian descent who identify anywhere on the LGBTQIA+, or queer, spectrum. We are a registered student organization and a QARC organization, as well as a member of the South Asian Caucus. We have meetings every other week with various events throughout the semester as well.

We are a student group acting independently of the University of California. We take full responsibility for our organization and this web site.
